Al Capone

American Gangster and Businessman

About Me

I'm Alphonse Gabriel Capone, better known as Al Capone or "Scarface". I was an American gangster who attained notoriety during the Prohibition era as the co-founder and boss of the Chicago Outfit.

My Enterprises

  • Bootlegging - Supplying and distributing illegal alcohol
  • Gambling - Operating speakeasies and illegal casinos
  • Racketeering - Providing "protection" services to businesses
  • Smuggling - Importing and distributing various contraband

Legacy

My name became synonymous with the gangster era of the 1920s and 1930s. Despite my notorious reputation, I was also known for my charisma and my contributions to the community, including opening one of the first soup kitchens during the Great Depression.
